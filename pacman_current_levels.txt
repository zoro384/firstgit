Pac-Man Championship Edition (Level 1)
Release Date: 2007
Key Features:

- Time-based gameplay with increasing speed.

Pac-Man Championship Edition DX (Level 2)
Release Date: 2010
Key Features:
- Ability to slow down time when close to enemies.
- Includes ghost trains that follow Pac-Man.

Pac-Man 256 (Level 3)
Release Date: 2015
Key Features:
- Endless maze gameplay.
- A glitch wave constantly pursues Pac-Man, inspired by the famous level 256 glitch.
